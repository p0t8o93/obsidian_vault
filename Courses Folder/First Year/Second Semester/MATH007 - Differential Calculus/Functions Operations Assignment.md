# Function Operations Assignment
Coverage is [[Operations and Properties of Functions|Function Operations]].

**Questions:**
Solve the following operations:
- $g(n)+h(n)$
- $g(t)-h(t)$
- $(g/h)n$
- $(g \cdot f )n$
- $g(h(t))$

Here are the given functions:
1) $$
	g(n)=n^{2}+4+2n
	$$
	
	$$
	h(n)=-3n+2
	$$
		
2) $$
	g(t)=t^{2}+3
	$$ 
	$$
	h(t)=4t-3
	$$
## Solution
A
$$
\begin{align}
g(n)+h(n)&=
n^{2}+2n+4+(-3n)+2 \\
&=n^{2}-n+6
\end{align}
$$

B
$$
\begin{align}
g(t)-h(t)&=
t^2+3-4t+3 \\
&=t^2-4t+6
\end{align}
$$

C
$$
(g/h)n
$$
