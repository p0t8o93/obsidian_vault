# Operations and Properties of Functions
## Operations
$$
f(x)=4x~~~~~~~~~g(x)=2x+3
$$
### Addition
$$
\begin{align}
f(x)+g(x)&=4x+2x+3 \\
&=6x+3
\end{align}
$$

### Subtraction
$$
\begin{align}
f(x)-g(x)&=4x-(2x+3) \\
&=2x-3
\end{align}
$$

### Multiplication
$$
\begin{align}
f(x)\times g(x)=(f \times g)(x) &=(4x)(2x+3) \\
&=8x^{2}+12x
\end{align}
$$
### Division
$f(x)\div g(x)=(f/g)(x)$

### Composition
$$
f(x)~\circ~g(x) \rightarrow f(g(x))
$$
